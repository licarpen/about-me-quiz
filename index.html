Bicycle and Pedestrian Infrastructure

The advent of driverless cars presents a unique opportunity to improve safety not only for passengers in cars, but for the cyclists and pedestrians who share the road. Transformative technologies could include

  • Moving cars 3 feet when passing a cyclist
  • Giving priority to bike/ped users at crossings
  • Rerouting bikes and cars to unique routes and lanes
  • Data collection

Access and Equity in Eduaction

Here are some ways to improve access to and equity in education

  1. Increase access to quality online resources
  2. Provide laboratory experience with augmented reality
  3. Connect students with quality instructors remotely

Computational Modeling

Many problems in physics are solved most easily but writing a computer program to simulate complex real world environments. Modeling helps account for variability and complexity, such as

  • Drag force
  • Interactions between multiple molecules
  • Elevation dependent acceleration due to gravity
